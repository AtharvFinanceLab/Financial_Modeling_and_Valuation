# Financial_mdeling_Valuation

7 financial models built from scratch across equity research, PE, M&A, real estate, project finance, and startup valuation.

Trent Ltd - Equity Research
Complete DCF and comps analysis on the listed retail stock in India.
Intrinsic value: ₹1,528 per share. CMP: ₹4,285.
Stock overvalued by 64%.

**LBO Mode:**
Private equity buyout analysis with multiple-tranche debt.
Investor achieves 31.2% IRR and 3.88x cash-on-cash over 5 years.

**M&A Model:**
Mergers and acquisitions analysis with synergies.
Transaction is 9.9% EPS accretive to the acquiring company.

**Real Estate Model:**
Rental property investment analysis with complete amortization over 30 years.
32.2% after-tax IRR with NPV of $886K.

**Solar Project Finance:**
Project finance analysis on a 50 MW solar plant.
12% equity IRR with DSCR of 1.53x – above threshold.

**Startup Valuation:**
Cohort-based SaaS valuation with complete LTV:CAC analysis and DCF.
Firm value: $425K. LTV:CAC ratio: 8.6x.

**Apollo Tyres - Governance Analysis:**
Management profiling and shareholding analysis of the top 11 executives.
No red flags were raised in the analysis.
